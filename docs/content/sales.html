Our Approach to Customer Success

At Kudoo, we believe that successful ISO 27001 implementation requires more than just great technology—it demands a partnership approach with knowledgeable experts who understand both information security and your business needs. Our dedicated sales and customer success teams work closely with you throughout your compliance journey, from initial exploration to certification and beyond.

Our customer-centric approach focuses on:

  • Understanding your unique compliance objectives and challenges
  • Tailoring our platform and services to your specific organizational needs
  • Providing expert guidance at every step of your implementation journey
  • Ensuring you achieve maximum value from your investment
  • Supporting your long-term compliance success through continuous improvement

We measure our success by your success—achieving and maintaining ISO 27001 certification efficiently while building a sustainable information security management system.

Our Sales Process

A Consultative Partnership

When you engage with Kudoo, you’re not just buying software—you’re partnering with compliance experts who care about your success. Our sales process is designed to be consultative, educational, and value-driven:

  1. Initial Discovery: We begin by understanding your organization’s specific compliance goals, challenges, timeline, and current state.

  2. Needs Assessment: Our team conducts a detailed evaluation of your requirements, identifying the optimal platform configuration and implementation approach.

  3. Tailored Demonstration: We provide a personalized walkthrough of the Kudoo platform, focusing on the capabilities most relevant to your specific needs.

  4. Solution Design: We develop a comprehensive implementation plan, including timelines, resource requirements, and key milestones.

  5. Investment Proposal: We provide transparent pricing and ROI projections that align with your business objectives and compliance requirements.

  6. Onboarding Plan: Upon agreement, we outline a detailed onboarding process to ensure a smooth transition to the Kudoo platform.

Our Commitment to You

  • True Expertise: Our sales team includes certified information security professionals who understand the nuances of ISO 27001.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your pricing model?

Our pricing is based on your organization’s size, complexity, and specific needs. We offer flexible annual subscription plans that include platform access, updates, and support. For detailed pricing information tailored to your organization, please request a consultation

How quickly can we implement ISO 27001 with Kudoo?

Implementation timelines vary based on your organization’s size, complexity, and starting point. Typically, our customers achieve certification readiness within 6-12 months. With our platform’s structured approach, many organizations see a 30-40% reduction in implementation time compared to traditional methods.

Do you offer a free trial of the platform?

While we don’t offer a self-service free trial, we provide comprehensive demonstrations and guided platform tours that allow you to experience Kudoo’s capabilities firsthand. Following your demonstration, we can create a limited test environment with your specific use cases to help evaluate the platform’s fit for your needs.

Can Kudoo replace our existing GRC tools?

Kudoo is specifically designed for ISO 27001 compliance, offering deeper functionality in this area than many general-purpose GRC tools. Many organizations use Kudoo alongside existing GRC platforms, while others choose to consolidate their compliance activities within our purpose-built solution. Our team can help assess your current toolset and identify the optimal approach.
