Kudoo Platform

Your complete solution for ISO 27001 compliance

Platform Overview

Kudoo transforms how organizations implement and maintain ISO 27001 compliance. Our platform brings together governance, risk, and compliance management into a unified solution built entirely within the Microsoft ecosystem, leveraging the tools your team already uses daily.

By integrating with Microsoft Dataverse, Power Platform, Teams, and SharePoint, we create a seamless experience that reduces the traditional complexity of compliance management. Whether you’re just beginning your ISO 27001 journey or looking to enhance your existing program, our platform provides the structure, guidance, and tools needed to achieve and maintain certification efficiently.

Core Capabilities

ISO 27001:2022 Framework

Our platform includes the complete ISO 27001:2022 standard with all 93 controls organized across four domains:

  • Organization (37 controls)
  • People (8 controls)
  • Physical (14 controls)
  • Technology (34 controls)

Each control comes with detailed descriptions, implementation guidance, and audit criteria to simplify your compliance journey.

Structured Implementation Approach

Our platform guides you through every phase of ISO 27001 implementation:

  1. Initial Assessment and Planning
  2. Risk Assessment and Treatment
  3. Implementation of Controls
  4. Training and Awareness
  5. Internal Audit and Review
  6. Certification Preparation
  7. Certification Audit
  8. Continuous Improvement

This phased approach ensures methodical progress toward certification without overwhelming your team.

Role-Based Implementation

Assign specific roles to team members with clear responsibilities:

  • Project Lead
  • Senior Management Representative
  • Domain Owners
  • Risk Owners
  • Internal Auditors

Each role receives tailored tasks, workflows, and dashboards to ensure everyone knows exactly what they need to do and when.

Policy Management

Create, store, manage, and distribute all required information security policies:

  • Information Security Policy
  • Access Control Policy
  • Risk Management Policy
  • And 15+ additional policy templates

All policies are mapped to specific ISO 27001 controls for complete traceability and compliance documentation.

Data Model & Architecture

Our platform is built on a structured data model that organizes all compliance elements in a logical, relational framework:

Governance Frameworks

The foundation of our platform, starting with ISO 27001:2022 and expandable to include other standards and regulations.

Domains

Logical groupings of controls by category (Organization, People, Physical, Technology) for better organization.

Controls

Individual requirements with detailed descriptions, implementation guidance, and audit criteria.

Checklists

Step-by-step implementation tasks for each control to guide the compliance process.

Policies

Documentation requirements mapped to specific controls with version management.

Roles

Defined responsibilities within the system with tailored views and permissions.

Tasks

Assigned activities with deadlines, descriptions, and linkage to specific controls.
