Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2025

Overview

At Kudoo, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our ISO 27001 compliance platform and visit our website.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account, request a demo, or contact us, we collect:

  • Name and contact details
  • Company information
  • Job title and role
  • Login credentials
  • Information you provide in communications with us

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or use our platform, we automatically collect:

  • Usage data (pages visited, features used)
  • Log data (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Device information
  • Cookies and similar technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Respond to comments, questions, and requests
  • Send administrative information
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Protect against malicious, deceptive, or illegal activity

Data Security

As an information security compliance platform, data security is at the core of our business. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Secure development practices
  • Compliance with ISO 27001 standards

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting, etc.)
  • In response to legal requirements
  • In connection with business transfers (merger, acquisition, etc.)
  • With your consent

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdrawal of consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@kudoo.io.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

International Data Transfers

As an Australian company serving global clients, we may transfer your information to other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for these transfers.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website or sending you an email.
