Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical demo last?

Our standard demonstrations take approximately 60 minutes, including time for questions and discussion. We can adjust the length based on your availability and the depth of information you require.

Who should attend the demo?

We recommend including representatives from your information security team, IT department, and compliance stakeholders. This ensures all relevant perspectives are considered during the demonstration.

Is there a cost for the demo?

No, our product demonstrations are completely free of charge and come with no obligation to purchase. We’re committed to helping you understand how Kudoo can benefit your organization.

Can we get a trial version after the demo?

Yes, following the demonstration, we can provide access to a trial environment where you can explore the platform further with sample data or your own information.

Microsoft Environment Requirements

For optimal use of Kudoo, your organization should have:

  • Microsoft 365 Business or Enterprise subscription
  • Microsoft Teams for collaboration features
  • SharePoint Online for document management
  • Power Platform access for customization (optional)
  • Microsoft Dataverse environment (we can help set this up)

During the demo, we can discuss your specific Microsoft environment and how Kudoo will integrate with your existing setup.
