Frequently Asked Questions

What’s included in the free Copilot app?

The free Kudoo Copilot app gives you access to our AI-powered compliance assistant within Microsoft Teams. It includes access to ISO 27001:2022 policy templates, implementation guidance for all 93 controls, and the ability to ask compliance-related questions. The free tier does not include the Power Platform dashboards, evidence management, or risk assessment tools.

Can I upgrade from Free to Standard later?

Yes! You can start with our free Copilot app and upgrade to our Standard plan at any time. When you upgrade, your existing policy templates and guidance can be integrated directly into the Power Platform, ensuring a seamless transition.

What determines the implementation cost?

Implementation costs vary based on several factors:

  • Your organization’s size and complexity
  • Your current state of ISO 27001 readiness
  • The scope of your Information Security Management System
  • Whether you need on-site or remote support
  • Your location and the specific implementation partner

Our implementation partners provide detailed quotes based on your specific requirements after an initial consultation.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations, educational institutions, and startups. Please contact our sales team to learn more about our discounting programs and eligibility requirements.

What happens if we need more than 25 users on the Standard plan?

The Standard plan includes up to 25 user accounts. If you need additional users, you can purchase add-on user packs or consider upgrading to our Enterprise plan, which includes unlimited users and additional features designed for larger organizations.

Do you offer a trial of the Standard or Enterprise plans?

While we don’t offer self-service trials of our paid plans, we do provide comprehensive demonstrations that show the full functionality of the platform with your specific use cases. After the demo, we can also set up a limited proof-of-concept environment to help you evaluate the platform’s capabilities.
