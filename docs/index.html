What is Kudoo?

Kudoo is a comprehensive Governance, Risk, and Compliance platform built within the Microsoft ecosystem. Our solution transforms how organizations implement and maintain ISO 27001 compliance by integrating with the tools your team already uses daily. With Kudoo, you can simplify complex compliance processes, reduce implementation time, and ensure continuous adherence to information security standards.

Our platform brings together all aspects of ISO 27001 compliance—from framework management and risk assessment to policy development and audit preparation—into a unified solution that leverages Microsoft Copilot, Dataverse, Power Platform, Teams, and SharePoint. This seamless integration creates an intuitive experience that reduces the traditional barriers to effective compliance management.

Key Platform Features

Policy Management

Create, store, and manage all required ISO 27001 policies with version control, approval workflows, and distribution tracking. Pre-built templates for over 20 essential information security policies accelerate your documentation process.

Risk Assessment & Treatment

Conduct comprehensive risk assessments with structured methodologies for identifying, analyzing, and evaluating information security risks. Develop and track risk treatment plans with clear ownership and monitoring of effectiveness.

Control Implementation

Track the implementation status of all 93 ISO 27001 controls with detailed checklists, evidence repositories, and progress dashboards. Assign responsibilities and monitor deadlines to ensure timely completion.

Audit Management

Plan and conduct internal audits with customizable audit checklists, finding tracking, and corrective action management. Prepare confidently for certification audits with evidence organization and readiness assessments.

Role-Based Implementation

Assign clear responsibilities to team members with role-specific dashboards, task lists, and notifications. From Project Lead to Domain Owners and Internal Auditors, everyone knows exactly what they need to do.

Reporting & Analytics

Gain visibility into your compliance program with executive dashboards, operational reports, and custom report builders. Track implementation progress, risk status, and audit findings with comprehensive analytics.
